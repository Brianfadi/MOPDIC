About Ministry of Planning, Development & International Cooperation

Driving sustainable development and economic growth in SSC-Khaatumo

Building a Better Future

Our Vision

The Ministry endeavors to establish itself as a leading state macro-economic planning body; dedicated to nurturing sustainable socio-economic advancement. Our vision revolves around elevating the collective well-being of SSC-Khaatumo's population while cultivating an atmosphere conducive to drawing substantial investments from local stakeholders and diaspora communities.

Our Mission

The Ministry is on a mission to excel in economic planning by crafting effective State development plans and strategies. We achieve this through rigorous macro-economic research, the implementation of sound policies, and the integration of advanced data and technology systems. Our goal is to drive efficient and impactful planning for the continuous advancement of our region.
