Welcome to Ministry of Planning and International Cooperation

The Ministry of Planning and International Cooperation (MOPIC) is responsible for planning, coordinating, and monitoring development programs and projects in Puntland State of Somalia. Our mission is to promote sustainable development, reduce poverty, and improve the quality of life for all citizens.

We work closely with government institutions, international partners, and local communities to implement development initiatives that align with the National Development Plan and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

To formulate, coordinate, and monitor development policies and programs that promote sustainable economic growth, social development, and poverty reduction in Puntland State of Somalia through effective planning, resource mobilization, and international cooperation.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in planning, coordinating, and implementing sustainable development initiatives that transform Puntland into a peaceful, prosperous, and globally competitive region with a high standard of living for all its citizens.
